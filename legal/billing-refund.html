Billing & Refund Policy

SportsSync subscriptions renew automatically until canceled.

All charges are processed by Stripe. You can cancel anytime from the billing portal. Cancellation applies at the end of the current billing period.

Unless required by law, fees are non-refundable and partial period credits are not issued.

Failed payment may place your account into grace status and then downgrade your plan to free limits if unresolved.
